About Me


Hello! I'm a junior at the University of Washington studying Computer Engineering. (Fun fact: I'm also minoring in Music.)

As a Seattle-based software engineer, I'm passionate about creating solutions that are smart, innovative, and effective. In the past, I've interned for GE Healthcare and the Department of Defense, working with relevant technologies to cut inefficiency and boost productivity.

I'm actively involved with the UW Chapter of the Society of Women Engineers as an executive officer and UNAVSA as Conference IT Staff.

When I'm not coding, I like to travel, take pictures (did you get to see my cover photo?), cheer for the Seahawks, try out different ethnic foods, engage with the Vietnamese-American community, and promote diversity in STEM fields.
